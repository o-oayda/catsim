- The S21_* mask is from Secrest et al. (2021), as used in Oayda & Lewis (2025).
  - Copied over from dipolesbi (21.11.25).
- The S22_* mask is from Secrest et al. (2022), as supplied by Mali. 
  - Converted from .pkl to .npy (21.11.25).

## General Notes
- Both maps are ring ordered and have nside=64.
- Both are binary masks, 1 being unmasked and 0 being masked.
- The S21 mask is in float dtype for whatever reason.
